Online class during Covid-19

Since I didn't have to significantly alter my daily schedule and the lecturer was accommodating and understanding during those times, I have enjoyed the switch from offline to online classes. This is because of the web conferencing platform's simplicity and effectiveness, the ability to stay in constant contact with classmates and professors via various channels, and the ability to continue taking classes while maintaining my current schedule.
